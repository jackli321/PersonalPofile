THE BAND

We love music

Jack (Yingda) Li was born in April 22, 1995. His hometown is call Taishan, which is a coastal city located at southern part of China. At the age of 10, he got his inspiration into music by Jay Chou’s song: Hair Like Snow (Fa Ru Xue). Which is a popular song at the mean time and shared by his cousin. Later on, he got chosen to be a part of the choirs of his elementary school. Two years later, he migrated to America with his parents and postponed his singing because he needed to focus on learning English. At the age of 15, he asked his parents that he wanted to attend a musical high school, but they rejected and signed up a guitar lesson for him as an exchange. Yet it was a classical guitar lesson and he did not have much interests to it so he quite the lesson and started to learn the acoustic guitar by self-taught. After he graduated from high school, he attended a community college for music major and got chosen to be the choirs to sang for the graduation ceremony for two years. At the meantime, he joined/co-founded couple local bands, started his journey with electric guitar, cooperated with New York University’s CSSA as a session guitarist, and co-hosted a singing show at Highline Ballroom (Show name: Understage), and performed with his band Spicy Jsus (former name: Noise project, Deng Deng Deng Deng). In 2018, he started to attend Berklee college of music and chose Contemporary Writing & Production as his major. He is dedicated to put more freshness into his music and be a one of a kind producer.
